# Call Intelligence Platform - Business Case Validation Questionnaire

## Purpose

This questionnaire is designed to validate or refute the key business assumptions outlined in the Business Case Assumptions document. Responses should be collected from target customers, industry experts, and key stakeholders.

## Section 1: Market & Customer Validation

**Objective**: Validate that the identified pain points are real and impactful.

**Question**: How significant is the problem of missed or poorly handled customer calls for your business?

## Scoring & Analysis Framework

### Data Collection Process

1. **Survey Distribution**:
   - 20-30 beta/early customers
   - 10-15 industry experts
   - 5-10 advisory board members
   - Total: 35-55 responses

2. **Weighting**:
   - Customer responses: 70% weight
   - Expert responses: 20% weight
   - Advisory board: 10% weight

3. **Validation Criteria**:
   - Hypothesis validated if >60% of weighted responses agree
   - Hypothesis partially validated if 40-60% agree
   - Hypothesis rejected if <40% agree

### Key Hypothesis Validation Checklist

- [ ] **Q1.1-1.2**: Call handling is critical problem (target: >70% "critical/significant")
- [ ] **Q1.3**: Coaching/quality is unmet need (target: >70% have "minimal/none")
- [ ] **Q2.1**: AI agent adoption comfort (target: >60% "very/somewhat comfortable")
- [ ] **Q3.1**: Pricing in $150-250 range acceptable (target: >60% select this range)
- [ ] **Q3.2**: ROI payback <6 months expected (target: >70%)
- [ ] **Q4.2**: Sales cycle <4 weeks (target: >60%)
- [ ] **Q5.2**: Vertical positioning matters (target: >70% "critical/important")
- [ ] **Q7.2**: Daily/weekly usage expected (target: >60%)
- [ ] **Q8.2**: NPS >45 predicted (target: >50% promoters)

### Post-Survey Actions

**If validated** (>60%):
- Proceed with confidence on this assumption
- Include in investor pitch / business plan
- Document supporting evidence

**If partially validated** (40-60%):
- Conduct additional research
- Consider product adjustments
- Include as risk factor in plan

**If rejected** (<40%):
- Major rethink required
- Pivot assumption or target customer
- Consider alternative product positioning
